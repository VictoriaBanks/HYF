Semantic Elements

A semantic element cleary describes describes its meaning to both the browser and the developer
Examples of semantic elements <form> <table> and <article> clearly defines its content
Semantic tags make it clear to the broswer what the meaning of a page and its content is
The clarity is also communicated with search engines ensuring that the right pages are delivered for the right queries
Examples of non semantic elemnts are <div> and <span> - tells nothing about its content
